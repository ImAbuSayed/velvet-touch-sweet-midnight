[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
[Acoustic guitar strums warm and inviting, mid-tempo]
I feel the embers burning in the dark
A slow fire catching in the shadows of my heart
Come a little closer, let the silence fade
This is the moment that the midnight made

[Verse 1]
[Smooth electric guitar lead enters gently]
I see the moonlight pouring through the glass
Dancing on your skin like a shadow on the grass
Every breath you take is pulling me right in
I want to lose myself where you and I begin

[Pre-Chorus]
[Bassline deepens, light piano chords]
The world outside is fading into gray
But everything is color when you look my way
A gentle shiver running down your spine
Your eyes are making promises and telling me you are mine

[Chorus]
[Full drum pocket, soaring atmospheric pads]
It is a velvet touch on a sweet midnight
Holding you close till the morning light
A tender fever underneath my skin
I will give you all my soul and let the magic win

[Post-Chorus]
[Organic percussion, acoustic rhythm]
Sweet midnight, falling into you
Sweet midnight, let the magic win

[Verse 2]
[Subtle atmospheric reverb, stripped-back acoustic feel]
The scent of autumn lingering in the air
I am tangled in the mystery, completely unaware
We do not need a compass, we do not need a guide
I found my only shelter right here by your side

[Pre-Chorus 2]
[Bassline deepens, light piano chords return]

A gentle shiver running down your spine

[Chorus]
[Full drum pocket, soaring atmospheric pads, emotional peak]
It is a velvet touch on a sweet midnight
Holding you close till the morning light
A tender fever underneath my skin
I will give you all my soul and let the magic win

[Post-Chorus 2]
[Organic percussion, acoustic rhythm]
Sweet midnight, falling into you
Sweet midnight, let the magic win

[Bridge]
[Stripped-back ambient textures, slow tempo]
The hours melt away into the deep unknown
A solitary rhythm that I call my own
We are tearing down the walls and breaking all the bars
A silent confession written in the stars
And even when the shadows start to slowly bleed
You will feel the steady heartbeat of Saeed

[Spoken]
[Music drops to silence]
[Whispered] (Let the morning wait for us)
[Whispered] (This night belongs to you and me)

[Final Chorus]
[Maximum emotional intensity, sweeping vocal harmonies]

Holding you close till the morning light
A tender fever underneath my skin
